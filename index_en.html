The company :

Dream Box is a start-up selling gift boxes of all types. The boxes are produced with respect for the environment, in fact cardboard is recycled and the ink is as natural as possible. Marketing Dream Box allows us to offer a unique customer experience.

Dream Box is a French company, all the boxes are manufactured in France.

Dream Box is a limited liability company (LLC).
